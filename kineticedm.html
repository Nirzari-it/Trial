Kinetic Super is thrilled to be joining forces with Sunsuper on 12 May 2018. The merger means we’ll be able to offer even more benefits to our members.

Sunny days ahead with Sunsuper

As one of Australia’s largest profit-for-member funds, Sunsuper offer:

  • More investment choice and competitive fees
  • Award-winning customer service*
  • A history of solid investment performance^
  • Access to qualified financial planners to help guide you to a brighter financial future

What you need to do

To make the transition as smooth as possible, we’ll automatically transfer your account across to Sunsuper on 12 May 2018. But there may be some actions you need to take depending on your circumstances, like:

  • Renewing binding beneficiary nominations and third-party authorities
  • Reviewing changes to insurance terms and conditions
  • Checking your new investment options
  • Understanding the new fees and costs

Getting to know your new account

It’s really important you read through the Significant Event Notice (SEN) carefully to understand the changes and ensure the move to Sunsuper is as sunny as it can be. We’ve also provided a handy checklist on page 31 of the SEN to help guide you through the key changes.

We’re here to help

Don’t hesitate to get in touch if you have any questions. We’re here to help.
